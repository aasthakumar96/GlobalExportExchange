How It Works

Our Service Process

A transparent, step-by-step process from initial inquiry to delivery & beyond.

Step 01
Initial Inquiry

You reach out via RFQ form or WhatsApp with your product requirements, quantity, destination, and timeline.

Step 02
Needs Assessment

We assess your requirements and identify the best sourcing options, pricing tiers, and trade terms within 24 hours.

Step 03
Quotation & Terms

You receive a detailed quotation with pricing in USD/INR, payment terms, lead time, and Incoterms.

Step 04
Order Confirmation

On your approval, we raise the Proforma Invoice. Payment via PayPal secures your order.

Step 05
Production & QC

We coordinate with the supplier, often conducting a pre-shipment inspection for larger orders.

Step 06
Shipment & Docs

Goods are dispatched with complete documentation. You receive tracking details and document copies via email.

Common Questions

Service FAQs

What Incoterms do you support?
We primarily work with EXW (Ex Works), FOB (Free on Board), and CIF (Cost, Insurance, Freight). The best Incoterm for your order depends on your logistics setup — we advise on this during quotation.
Do you ship to all countries?
We ship globally. However, some products have export restrictions or require additional permits for specific destinations. We advise on this during the inquiry stage to ensure full compliance.
Can I request a sample before placing a bulk order?
Yes! Samples can be arranged for most product categories. Sample costs vary by product and are typically charged to the buyer. Contact us via WhatsApp or the RFQ form to request.
What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?
MOQ varies significantly by product category and supplier. Some products can be ordered in small trial quantities; others (especially bulk commodities) have larger minimum volumes. Submit an RFQ for product-specific MOQ information.
How long does it take to get a quote?
For standard products, we typically provide a preliminary quote within 24–48 hours. For custom or OEM orders, we may need 3–5 working days to confirm pricing with the supplier.

# Global Export Exchange (GEE)

**Global Export Exchange (GEE)** is a premier international trade partner based in Gurgaon, Haryana. We specialize in connecting Indian manufacturers, MSMEs, and producers with global markets through a suite of AI-driven tools, strategic networking, and end-to-end export-import solutions.

## 🚀 Our Focus
- **Ayurvedic & Food Products:** Grains, spices, supplements, and wellness products.
- **Energy Commodities:** Crude Oil, LNG, and petroleum byproduct trade facilitation.
- **Essential Oils:** Pure natural extracts and aromatherapy for global industries.
- **Handicraft Items:** Unique Indian decor, metalware, and artisan products.
- **Trade Promotion:** B2B/B2G meetings, MSME support, and AI-driven business growth.

## 📍 Presence
- **Headquarters:** Yoffice Complex, Sector-45 (Vill-Kanhai), Gurgaon, Haryana, India.
- **Reach:** Global shipping and trade facilitation via strategic freight partnerships.

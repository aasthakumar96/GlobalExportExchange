Who We Are

India's Trusted Bridge to Global Markets

Global Export Exchange (GEE) is a Gurgaon-based export-import-trade company dedicated to connecting international buyers with premium Indian commodities. We specialize in everything from Crude Oil & LNG to Ayurvedic products and handicrafts.

Our mission is to foster global trade through B2B/B2G meetings, MSME support, and AI-based business growth strategies. Headquartered in Haryana, we simplify international commerce for partners worldwide.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To become India's most trusted and transparent export exchange — a global marketplace where quality, integrity, and speed define every trade relationship.

We envision a world where small and mid-size buyers anywhere can access India's vast manufacturing and agricultural potential without barriers, delays, or doubt.

Our Mission

To simplify international trade from India by providing end-to-end export solutions — from product sourcing and quality verification to documentation, logistics, and after-shipment support.

Every buyer who works with us should feel confident, informed, and well-served from their first inquiry to final delivery.
